Track your plants with the click of a button

BotaniClock is a device designed to help you track the last time you watered your plant and to provide that information to you via this website.

Your plants will thank you

Overwatering cacti? Underwatering impatiens? Using the power of BotaniClock, you'll always know exactly when it's time to water your plant next, even whenever life gets busy.
